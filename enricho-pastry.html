Enrico pastry
Decades of sweetness in the heart of Addis

Enrico, the Italian bakery in Addis Ababa's old town, has a remarkable history that spans over a century. Established in 1946 by Signore Enrico Lumini, the bakery has been serving delectable pastries to its customers since its inception. It has garnered immense admiration and loyalty from its patrons.
Enrico holds a special place in Addis Ababa's history, as it has been involved in significant events. One such event was the preparation of a birthday cake for Emperor Haile Selassie's 80th birthday, which took place at Mazegaja Bet. This bakery has become a monument to the city's past, symbolizing its cultural and historical significance.

Enrico's interior reflects a cozy and inviting atmosphere, with warm colors, traditional Italian bakery displays, and the aroma of freshly baked goods wafting through the air.it is often packed with eager customers. The bakery's decor combines classic elements with a touch of modernity, creating a charming and comfortable space for customers. Enrico remains open today, continuing to serve its loyal and satisfied customers. Its operating hours are from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm, Monday to Saturday, with shorter hours on Sundays.

One of the reasons for Enrico's popularity is its timeless recipes, which have been passed down through generations. These cherished recipes contribute to the bakery's reputation for consistent quality and taste. Additionally, Enrico is known for offering a wide variety of pastries at affordable prices, making it accessible to a diverse range of customers.

Enrico's commitment to quality is evident in every aspect of their baking process. They use only the finest ingredients, ensuring that each pastry is made with care and attention to detail. Whether it's the flaky crust of a croissant or the velvety texture of a cream-filled pastry, Enrico's dedication to quality shines through.
