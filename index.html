The Emergence of Skateboard Culture
In Addis

Skateboarding has become more than just a recreational activity in Ethiopia; it has become a means of empowerment and community building. Skate parks, such as Addis Skatepark in Addis Ababa, have become focal points for young skaters to gather, connect, and share their passion for the sport. These spaces provide an environment that fosters creativity, self-expression, and mutual support.

The Ethiopian Coffee Ceremony: A Ritual of Rich Tradition and Aromatic

It is a time-honored practice that transcends the mere act of brewing and sipping coffee—it is a social event that celebrates friendship, conversation, and the simple pleasures of life.
