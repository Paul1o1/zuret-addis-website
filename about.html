About Us And Our Team

Who.

Zuret Addis is a leading company based in Addis Abeba, Ethiopia promoting and providing the ultimate Addis Ababa experience for its people.

When.

Zuret Addis was established in 2019 and it is still going strong. Overtime we have become well-versed in the city's hidden gems and can offer valuable insights to enhance clients' experiences.

What We Do.

We have been promoting Addis Ababians on Social Media and have been experimenting on new ways of showing the city to foreigners and locals.

What to Expect.

On the Coming Months, we will be releasing products that we think will redefine Media, Publishing and Tourism in Addis Ababa and are excited to bring freshness to the city.
